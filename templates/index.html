How To Use Smart Signal Pro

Complete guide to reading signals, placing trades, and managing risk

01

03

The Perfect Trade Checklist

Only take a trade when ALL green conditions are met. Skip any trade with a red condition.

✓ Signal is BUY or SELL — never trade WAIT
✓ Strength is 75% or above
✓ Risk shows LOW (MEDIUM is acceptable)
✓ Confirmations are 6 or more
✓ Trend matches signal direction
✓ Heiken Ashi matches signal
✗ Do NOT trade HIGH RISK signals
✗ Do NOT trade during major news (NFP, rate decisions)
✗ Do NOT trade if signal just refreshed — wait 60 seconds
04

Step-by-Step Trade Entry

1
Open Dashboard — select 5M timeframe
2
Find a BUY or SELL card with strength ≥75% and LOW risk
3
Click the card — check the Indicator panel. Confirm 6+ indicators agree
4
Note the entry advice shown on the card (e.g. "Enter NOW")
5
Open your broker (Quotex/IQ Option) — select the same asset
6
Set expiry to 5 minutes (or match your timeframe)
7
BUY signal → press UP ▲  |  SELL signal → press DOWN ▼
8
After expiry — mark WIN or LOSS in the Signal History table
05

What Each Indicator Means

RSIBelow 30 = oversold → expect price bounce UP. Above 70 = overbought → expect price drop DOWN.
MACDWhen MACD line crosses above signal = bullish momentum (BUY). Crosses below = bearish (SELL).
BollingerPrice touching lower band = bounce expected (BUY). Price at upper band = reversal expected (SELL).
StochasticK below 20 = oversold zone (BUY). K above 80 = overbought zone (SELL). Both K and D must agree.
EMA/SMAFast EMA above slow SMA = uptrend active (BUY). Below = downtrend active (SELL).
Heiken Ashi2+ consecutive green HA candles = strong uptrend. 2+ red = strong downtrend. Very reliable confirmation.
S/R LevelPrice near support = likely bounce up. Price near resistance = likely reversal down. Price loves these zones.
Candle PatternHammer/Engulfing at support = strong BUY. Shooting Star/Engulfing at resistance = strong SELL.
06

Money Management — Most Important

✓ DO THIS
Bet only 2–3% of balance per trade
On ₹10,000 → max ₹200–300 per trade
Max 3 trades per session
Stop after 2 losses in a row — take a break
Stop if you lose 10% of your balance in one day
Only trade signals with 75%+ strength
✗ NEVER DO THIS
Never double your bet to recover a loss (Martingale)
Never trade with money you need for living
Never trade more than 3 times in a row
Never trade HIGH RISK signals
Never trade against the trend direction
⚠ OTC pairs have no real market — the broker controls the price. Trade real forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD) when possible for more reliable signals.
